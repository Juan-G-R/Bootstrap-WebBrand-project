Meets

Madrid 21 sept meet

On a sunny Madrid afternoon, JDM car enthusiasts gathered in a parking lot filled with Honda, Toyota, Nissan, and more. Engines roared, tires screeched, and the passion for Japanese cars filled the air. It was a showcase of creativity and customization in the capital.

Sevilla 18 oct meet

In the picturesque setting of Seville, a gathering of motor enthusiasts took place, but this time, the spotlight was on the world of classic cars. Iconic cars from different eras filled the city's main square as enthusiasts shared stories and admired the timeless elegance of these vehicles. It was a day where the past and present came together in an atmosphere of appreciation for automotive history.

Murcia 15 june meet

In the charming city of Murcia, an exhilarating car racing event took place at the famous local circuit. From high-end sports cars to rally vehicles, the track was the stage for fierce and thrilling competition. Engines roared, tires screeched, and the crowd cheered for their favorite drivers as they tackled the circuit's twists and straightaways. It was a day filled with adrenaline and excitement for speed enthusiasts in Murcia.

Barcelona 4 nov meet

In Barcelona, an exciting gathering of car enthusiasts brought together an impressive array of vehicles in a bustling city location. Engines roared, and enthusiasts shared their love for automobiles, creating an exhilarating event.
